HOW TO PLAY

CREDITS

In order to get the airplane flying you can use the "W-A-S-D" or Arrow configuration. In particular, the controls are:
"A" or Left-Arrow to move left the plane;
"D" or Right-Arrow to move right the plane;
"W" or Up-Arrow to move up the plane;
"S" or Down-Arrow to move down the plane;
"Q" to flip left;
"E" to flip right;
